Registration Process

Student registration is an easier and much faster way for college representatives to follow up with you after the fair. You won't have to stand in line or fill out information cards, allowing you to focus on finding the right school for you!

  1. Visit www.gotomyncf.com.
  2. Select the fair you wish to attend.
  3. Complete all required fields and submit your information.
  4. Print out your barcode and BRING it to the fair!

Once you register, there is no need to stand in the student registration line at the fair!

NACAC doesn not sell your information to third parties. NACAC is committed to maintaining high standards that foster ethical and social responsibility, as outlined in the NACAC Statement of Principles of Good Practice (SPGP).
